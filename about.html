About AKAH Logistics

AKAH Logistics is built on reliability, integrity, and professional service. The name stands for African King American Hustler — a mindset rooted in excellence and ambition.

Founded by Cyrus Kulee, our mission is simple: provide delivery services customers can trust daily.

We specialize in same-day routes, scheduled pickups, medical deliveries, and small-business logistics support.

At AKAH Logistics, we don’t just move packages — we deliver peace of mind.

Trusted. Efficient. Driven. The AKAH Way.
